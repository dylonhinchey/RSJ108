How to be a broadcast journalist ...


Are you wondering how to become a broadcast journalist? Take a look at the following steps.

  • Simple Steps

  • Take Journalism classes
  • Watch and study different news broadcasts
  • Apply for internships
  • Once you have an internship, learn as much as possible from it!
  • Turn that internship into a job.

  1. Rejection can happen, do no give up though.

  2. Keep learning and working hard!

  3. Here is a video showing 5 tips on how to become a broadcast journalist.

    "It's not about being on TV"

  4. It's about reporting news!

  5. Be yourself, care about your work and the stories you're telling.
